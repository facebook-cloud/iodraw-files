```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant pc as PC端工具
    participant user as adb/uart登录用户
    participant tbox as 车端TBox
    participant tech_center as 动态密码机制-技术中心
  

    user->>tbox: 请求登录
    tbox->>tbox: 生成挑战码CC [1]
    pc->>tbox: 用公钥加密CC, 得到NumCC (Base64编码)
    tbox->>tech_center: 发送NumCC给技术中心
    tech_center->>tech_center: 载入NumCC
    tech_center->>tech_center: 调用CA解密接口 (NumCC)
    tech_center->>tech_center: 解密NumCC得到CC
    tech_center->>tech_center: 根据CC计算得到登录口令Pwd [2]
    tech_center->>user: 将Pwd告知用户
    user->>tbox: 手动输入口令Pwd
    tbox->>tbox: 验证口令 [3]
    tbox->>tbox: 验证成功?
    alt 验证成功
        tbox->>tbox: 进入系统
    else 验证失败
        tbox->>tbox: 流程结束 (返回)
    end


```
